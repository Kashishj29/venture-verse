About VentureVerse

ML-powered startup success prediction using pre-launch public indicators.

What It Does

Analyses early-stage indicators — funding, investors, network, milestones, industry, location — to estimate success probability before market entry.

Free and open-access, built on real Crunchbase data.

⚠️ Dataset Notice: VentureVerse's model was trained on US-based Crunchbase startup data (pre-2014). Predictions are indicative for non-US users. Ecosystem categories are used to approximate global startup environments.
